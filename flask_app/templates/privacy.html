Privacy Policy

The ASSAS Data Hub is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your personal data:

  • Usage Data: We may log access times, IP addresses, and actions for security and platform improvement.
  • Data Storage: Your data is stored securely and only accessible to authorized users and administrators.
  • Data Sharing: Personal data is not shared with third parties except as required by law or project agreements.
  • Cookies: The platform may use cookies for authentication and session management.
  • Data Removal: You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting support.
  • Contact: For privacy questions, email support.

This policy may be updated periodically. Please review it regularly.
